# Privacy Policy for ExaCode

**Last updated:** January 27, 2026

## Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the ExaCode mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Wa'el Almomani (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service.

This policy is intended to inform users of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information if anyone decides to use our Application.

## Information Collection and Use

The Application does **NOT** collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information or user data with external servers or third parties.

### Data Stored Locally

The Application stores the following data **locally on your device only** using Hive database:

- **Quiz Results:** Category, difficulty level, score, total questions, percentage, and date/time of each quiz attempt
- **Application Preferences:** User interface settings (if any)

This data is stored exclusively on your device and is **never transmitted** to any external server.

## No Internet Connection Required

ExaCode is a **100% offline application**. It does not require an internet connection to function and does not communicate with any external servers.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Only you have access to your quiz history and results
- No data is shared with the Service Provider or any third parties
- You can delete all stored data by clearing the app's data in your device settings

## No Third-Party Services

The Application does **NOT** use:
- Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
- Advertising services (e.g., AdMob, Facebook Ads)
- Crash reporting services (e.g., Crashlytics, Sentry)
- Social media integrations (e.g., Facebook Login, Google Sign-In)
- Cloud storage or backup services
- Any third-party services that collect, store, or process user data

The Application operates entirely offline and does not connect to any external servers or APIs.

## Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected or transmitted to external servers, it is safe for users of all ages, including children. 

Parents and guardians should be aware that all quiz results and statistics are stored locally on the device and can be reviewed or deleted at any time through the app settings or device settings.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with any information, please contact us, and we will take steps to ensure that such information is deleted from our records (if any inadvertently exists).

## Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by:
1. Using the "Clear History" option within the app
2. Clearing the app's data in your device settings
3. Uninstalling the application

The Service Provider may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
